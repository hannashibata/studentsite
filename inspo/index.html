Typography

These are the links for nice typeface other than Google Fonts. (Google Fonts are great but tends to be over-used.)

Open Source:

  • Studio Feixen: Studio Feixen is a swiss based design company. They are committed to creating good looking stuff to make the world a better place.
  • Extraset: Extraset is an independent Swiss type foundry established in Geneva.
  • Collletttivo: Collletttivo is an Open-Source type foundry established in 2017 in Milan, a network of people promoting the practice of type design through mutual exchange and collaboration.
  • Bad Type Club: Bad Type Club is an experimental type foundry established in 2017, located in Covington, Kentucky.

CLosed Source Types:

  • Adobe Fonts: Who doesn’t go to Adobe Fonts.
  • Pangram Pangram: Pangram Pangram was established in 2018. Located in Canada. Their PP Editorial Old is used in this site!
  • Grilli Type: Grilli Type is founded in late 2009, located in Switzerland. I love GT Alpina.
  • Heofler&Co: Who doesn’t admire Hoefler.
  • Klim Typ eFoundry (Klim): Founded in 2005 and is based in Te Whanganui-a-Tara/Wellington, Aotearoa/New Zealand. Pitch is a cute family.
  • Lost Type: Operated and Managed by Riley Cran (and originally co-founded with Tyler Galpin), Lost Type is the first of its kind, a Pay-What-You-Want type foundry.
